Liquidity

4 million tokens, or 40% of the total supply, will be allocated for liquidity. These tokens will be made available for trading on tinyman, allowing for easy buying and selling of Rabbit Coin..

Staking and Farming

3 million tokens, or 30% of the total supply, will be allocated for staking and farming. These tokens will be used to incentivize community members to hold and stake Rabbit Coin, providing a mechanism for increasing the value of the token..

Campaign and Community

Rabbit Coin is allocating 1 million tokens, or 10% of its total supply, for campaigns and community incentives, which will be used to reward community members for their contributions, such as promoting the coin, creating memes, and participating in community events.

Team Alocation

1 million tokens, or 10% of the total supply, will be allocated for the team. These tokens will be used to incentivize the team to continue developing and promoting Rabbit Coin.

Product Development

1 million tokens, or 10% of the total supply, will be allocated for product development. These tokens will be used to fund the development of new features and products related to Rabbit Coin.

It is also worth noting that team tokens and part of community and product development tokens will be locked for 4 months along with liquidity.
